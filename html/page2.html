SP's Achievements


Awards in Competitions :

  • SP teams have consistently excelled in competitions like the International Standards Olympiad, where they secured Silver and Bronze awards in the 17th edition, and multiple top prizes in earlier editions.

Innovation and Entrepreneurship :

  • SP students and alumni have successfully founded startups and received accolades such as the National Youth Entrepreneurship Award Gold Award.

Technology and Engineering :

  • SP teams have won prestigious awards for their projects in assistive and rehabilitation technology, including Gold and Best Presentation Awards at the National Assistive and Rehabilitation Technology Student Innovation Challenge.

Future Plans



Smart Campus Initiative

SP is transforming into a Smart Campus, leveraging technology to enhance teaching, learning, and campus management. This initiative includes using video analytics for security and personalized services through advanced wireless setups. Students are actively involved in this transformation through initiatives like the Smart Poly Awards, which encourage innovative projects such as the "Smart Mouse Trap"

Sustainability Efforts

SP is committed to significant sustainability goals. Their "60-30 Vision" aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 60% by 2030, based on 2022 levels. They have also partnered with KPMG to develop a comprehensive net-zero sustainability roadmap by 2024, contributing to the decarbonization efforts in higher education institutions

Educational and Community Engagement

The institution continues to emphasize holistic and industry-relevant education, supported by programs such as the Idea Grants Fund, which enables faculty to pursue innovative ideas aligned with the Smart Campus initiative. They also focus on community engagement through various collaborative projects and initiatives aimed at reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainability.
